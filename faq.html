Frequently Asked Questions
Can men join UQWN and attend the events?
Certainly! We encourage all UQ men interested in pushing for diversity to attend the UQWN events. Creating true gender parity requires effort from both men and women.
What is the best way to find out about the upcoming UQWN events and opportunities?
Like our Facebook page to keep up to date with all our latest event, sponsor opportunities and more!
When are you taking committee applications?
At the start and middle of each year we will open sub-executive applications. Keep an eye out on our Facebook page!
