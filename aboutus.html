What we do

The UQ Women’s Network (UQWN) was founded in December 2019. We are the University of Queensland’s first multi-disciplinary student society, dedicated to the success of emerging young women. The UQWN strives to cultivate strong women of tomorrow, by serving as a platform for enterprising young women across the university. In 2020 we will bring you inspiring speakers, provide workshops for skill development, connect you with like-minded students and provide you with access to new opportunities.
