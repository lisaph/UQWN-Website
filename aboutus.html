What we do
The UQ Women’s Network (UQWN) was founded in December 2019. We are the University of Queensland’s first multi-disciplinary student society, dedicated to the success of young women. The UQWN will provide members with inspirational speakers, workshops for skill development and connect members with like-minded students.

Through this exposure, the UQWN will equip the future female leaders with the confidence and the necessary skills and network to pursue opportunities within male-dominated fields.
